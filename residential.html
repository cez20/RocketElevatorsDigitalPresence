Residential Services

At Rocket Elevators, we offer both low-trip and mid-trip elevators. We sincerely believe that our service offering covers all of your elevator needs for your personal residence.

Low-trip elevators

In low buildings up to seven floors, Rocket Elevators offers a variety of passenger transport solutions. And for us, low does not mean low cost, Rocket Elevators has a full range of services for low-rise buildings, always ensuring safety, comfort and energy efficiency. With installations with or without a machine room, the space saving is always taken into account. Quality and technology are maintained at all price points and options. Contact us to find out more about how Rocket Elevators can become your supplier of low elevators.

Mid-trip elevators

Rocket Elevators is uniquely positioned to understand this average size of buildings that are being built around the world. With our Gen2 Elevator technology, we promise comfort, reliability and quality at all prices. You know our experience and knowledge in providing passenger transport solutions for countless tall and innovative buildings. We translate this into a high quality solution for mid-sized buildings offering an amazing experience in tall buildings. In addition to this, our Compass destination management software is available at the mid-level.
